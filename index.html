You are headed to Portland. You should know:

  • It's illegal to pump your own gas in Oregon, as well as to use canned corn as fish bait, or box with a kangaroo. Also, it's illegal to possess an uncured animal hide in Portland city limits, or to whistle under water. You've been warned.
  • Portland was not, in fact, discovered by Lewis and Clark. It was discovered by a lot of Californians who got tired of Bay Area traffic and high housing costs.
  • We eat a lot of Voodoo Donuts and drink a lot of Stumptown Coffee - but it doesn't show. Also, we can drink beer at our movie theaters.
  • There's no sales tax in Oregon, which is good, because we aren't particularly good at math. We are good at riding bikes.

  • PDX is the 23rd largest city in the U.S, but we drink more locally brewed beer than any city in the world (trust us, we're drinking right now).
  • Portland is not the capital of Oregon, but it is the capital of the Hipster phenomenon. (Not Brooklyn or Austin - just count our mustaches and tattoos.) Also, Oregon residents own 1/4th of the U.S.' llama population.

Bridgetown brings Drupal

Yes, we're putting a bird on Drupal. Join us in welcoming the international Drupal community to the "Silicon Forest" May 20-24, 2013, for DrupalCon Portland. Never been to Portland? Well, we think we're pretty cool. Coffee. Beer. Bikes. Mountains. Mustaches. Salmon. Bridges. Roses. OSCON. And lots and lots of open source.
